How to trade-in your books?

Rather than sell your book one by one, trade-in your books with us and get a fair price. For any queries, contact us at bookcyle.sustainable@gmail.com.

  1. Take photos of your book on the front, bookspint and back
  2. Send us an email at bookcycle.sustainable@gmail.com
  3. In the email, provide information as such: book title, pictures of the book and a description to note any wear and tear.
  4. After receiving the email, we will take 7 working days to reply with an estimated quotation for the books that we will take in
  5. You can choose to accept or decline. If accept, we will arrange a time for us to pick up the books at your convenience. Pick-up cost will be free for trade-in above $100. Below that, we will inform you beforehand
  6. Once we have collected the books, we will vet them for quality assurance and if it is certified to meet our standards, we will list them on our platform
  7. Once the book is being sold, we will transfer you the money via bank transfer.
